From zero to hero in 18 months

If you want to become rich, quickly and with no effort. You are in the right place.

The background

I was born and raised in a poor region of the world, but I had always dreamed of becoming wealthy.

The idea

One day, while walking near my house, I stumbled upon a rock and had a sudden epiphany. Why not adopt a rock as a pet? After all, pebbles were cute, didn't make noise, didn't require any maintenance, and were free.

The flourishing business

I immediately started my own business selling pebbles as pets and it quickly took off. People loved the idea and I generated $250k ARR in no time. My business was a huge success and I soon found myself a self-made millionaire.

The apogee

But my success story didn't end there. I eventually sold my business for a whopping 10.4 million dollars to my father, who was at the time the CEO of Bouygues Construction. With the money, I was able to provide a better life for my family and fulfill my dream of becoming wealthy. My journey from a young man from a poor region of the world to a successful entrepreneur is a testament to the power of a creative idea and hard work. I proved that with a little bit of imagination and determination, anyone can become a success, no matter where they come from.
